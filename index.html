Services

Here are various dragon services that we offer. They are all free, both as in freedom and price. Most of these services can interact with other services

Matrix

A decentralized, encrypted and federated chatting platform.

Fediverse

Fediverse is a social media network which doesn't depend on a centralized server.

Peertube

An independent fediverse compatible video sharing platform that anyone can host.

SearXNG

A simple and private search that indexes results from various other search engines like Google, Bing, Duckduckgo, etc...

Forgejo

Forgejo is an easy and selfhostable git interface.

Invidious

A lightweight Youtube front-end which doesn't have any ads, tracking. This front-end doesn't require JavaScript.
